I am a fourth-year CS PhD student at the [University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu/). I am a member of [CreateLab](https://createlab.cs.illinois.edu/) advised by [Prof. Yongjoo Park](https://cs.illinois.edu/about/people/faculty/yongjoo). My research interests lie within **Database Systems** on the topics of **Data management** and **Support for ML/AI**.

My current work:
 - Workload-aware Composite Vector Indexing
 - Kishu: fast incremental checkpointing and versioning for machine learning and EDA notebook workloads

My previous research projects:
